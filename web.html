An e-blood bank management system is a digital platform used to manage the storage, inventory, and distribution of blood products within a blood bank or blood donation center. It allows for the efficient tracking and management of blood products from the time of donation to the time of transfusion, helping to ensure the safety and quality of the blood supply. Some of the key features of an e-blood bank management system include: Blood testing and processing: This module includes functions for managing blood testing and processing, including tracking test results and ensuring that blood products meet safety and quality standards. Transfusion management: This module includes functions for managing the transfusion of blood products, including tracking patient information, blood product orders, and transfusion records. Overall, an e-blood bank management system can help to streamline blood bank operations, reduce errors, and improve patient safety by ensuring that the right blood products are available at the right time and that they meet safety and quality standards.

Blockchain is a decentralized digital ledger technology that allows for secure, transparent, and tamper-proof recording of transactions or data. It consists of a distributed database that is shared among a network of computers or nodes, and each block in the chain contains a record of multiple transactions. Once a block is added to the chain, it cannot be altered or deleted, ensuring the integrity of the data stored on the blockchain. Transactions on the blockchain are verified by a network of users, rather than a centralized authority, making it a trustless system.
